WHO WE ARE
Auokka is developing a ecosystem to provide continue education for people who willing to develop in-demand skills by learning from experts. We are establishing partnership with training institutes, enterprises and universities to offer quality short-courses in IT industry. And also developing a teaching team consist with professionals/experts with various skillset and knowledge which cover software development, data analytics, AI and Machine Learning.
AT AUOKKA WE PROVIDE
  • Continuing Education for people to develop in-demand skills by learning from experts
  • Eco-system to bridge fresh graduates and various industries
  • Management platform to ensure course quality for student, trainer, institute, university and enterprise
  • Open community to share experience and FAQ: technical questions, career opportunity, start-up etc…
